About Us

Virtual data for information technology

Virtual Data Company for Information Technology is a leading Jordanian software development company that was established in 2019. We aim to create a distinctive footprint in the field of information technology. Today, we take pride in having over 600,000 users who utilize our various products. We are currently present in three Arab countries: Jordan, Egypt, and Palestine. Our plan is to expand and open more branches to reach broader markets in the region and globally, in line with our company's five-year vision.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission: We're Virtual Data IT, a Jordanian software firm founded in 2019, specializing in advanced software for individuals and businesses. We focus on enhancing lives and adding value across various sectors.

Vision: To become the leading company in the field of software development in the Middle East, and to expand globally to meet the needs of customers around the world.
